Resizable

What?

Easily make a div element resizable.

I'm contained in my parent!
Resize me!

How?

<link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="resizable.css">
[...]
<div class="well">
    <div class="well" ui-resizable>
        <div class="well" ui-resizable="{containment: 'parent'}">I'm contained in my parent!</div>
        Resize me!
    </div>
</div>
